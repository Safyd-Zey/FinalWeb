Spain

Football in Spain is a national passion. The national team is a three-time European champion and one-time World Cup winner. La Liga, particularly involving Real Madrid and Barcelona, is one of the strongest leagues in the world. This game has become an integral part of Spanish culture, uniting people from different walks of life.
